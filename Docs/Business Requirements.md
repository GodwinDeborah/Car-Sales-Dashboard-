# Car Sales Dashboard | Business Requirement Document (BRD)

## Project Overview

The Car Sales Dashboard is designed to provide a centralized view of vehicle sales performance across multiple models, manufacturers, and regions. Developed in Tableau, the dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key sales metrics, analyze performance trends, and gain actionable insights through interactive data visualizations.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Tableau dashboard that provides stakeholders with a comprehensive view of vehicle sales performance. The dashboard will track key sales indicators, monitor year-over-year growth, analyze customer purchasing patterns, and support data-driven decision-making through intuitive reporting and visual analytics.

## Business Requirements

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard should provide a high-level overview of sales performance through the following core metrics:

#### Sales Performance

* Year-to-Date (YTD) Total Sales
* Year-over-Year (YOY) Growth in Total Sales

#### Average Price Performance

* YTD Average Selling Price
* YOY Growth in Average Selling Price

#### Vehicle Sales Metrics

* YTD Cars Sold
* YOY Growth in Cars Sold

## Chart Requirements

### YTD Sales Weekly Trend

A line chart displaying weekly YTD sales performance to identify sales patterns, seasonality, and fluctuations over time.

### YTD Total Sales by Body Style

A visual breakdown of YTD total sales across vehicle body styles to evaluate customer purchasing preferences and product demand.

### YTD Total Sales by Color

A donut chart illustrating the contribution of vehicle colors to overall YTD sales performance.

### YTD Cars Sold by Dealer Region

A bar chart comparing vehicle sales across dealer regions to highlight geographic performance trends and regional sales distribution.

### Company-Wise Sales Trend

A tabular view presenting sales performance by manufacturer, enabling comparison of YTD sales figures across companies.

## Dashboard Filters

To support detailed analysis and improve user interaction, the dashboard includes the following filters:

* Date
* Transmission Type
* Vehicle Body Style
* Engine Type
* Customer Gender

## Expected Business Outcomes

The dashboard is expected to:

* Provide a centralized and interactive view of vehicle sales performance.
* Improve visibility into sales trends and growth opportunities.
* Support analysis of customer purchasing behavior across vehicle categories.
* Enable performance comparisons across manufacturers and dealer regions.
* Facilitate faster and more informed business decisions through data-driven insights.

## Conclusion

The Car Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive framework for monitoring and analyzing vehicle sales performance. By combining key performance indicators, trend analysis, regional comparisons, and customer-focused insights, the dashboard enables stakeholders to better understand sales dynamics and identify opportunities for growth. Through interactive filtering and visual reporting, the solution supports efficient decision-making and enhances overall business visibility.
